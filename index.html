About

I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Statistics & Data Science at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), where I am super fortunate to be advised by Prof. Oscar Hernan Madrid Padilla. Before my Ph.D., I received my bachelor’s degree in Economics with a minor in Data Science from Fudan University.

My current research focuses on developing deep-learning-based statistical methods with theoretical guarantees, with broader interest in nonparametric statistics. I also enjoy collaborating on and solving various real-world problems, including an interesting project on chimpanzees!
